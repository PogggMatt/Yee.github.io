Text-to-Text

There are many interesting world ending movies, the whole purpose of this type of movie is to warn humans. The view from this story is that three robots travel the wreckage of New York city. Others of the same type of story usually have views from humans, but “Three Robot” chooses to use the view from god, which is the three robots viewing and commenting about humans. Usually in these movies, humans have a good ending, they always have a main character to save the world, but in this world, humans have already ended. In real life, humans don’t have a main character to save the world, all we can do is to protect the environment.

The three robots are like the alien that watches us every day, the alien that just watches us silently. Humans are so arrogant and that makes them destroyed by themselves, the robot says. We can see that humans from now on don't really care about the natural environment, we use all the resources from Earth. Humans think they are the top predator on earth, which make them arrogant.
